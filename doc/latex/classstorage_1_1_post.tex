\subsection{\-Detailed \-Description}
\-Used to hold a thread and all it's associated comments.\par
 \par
 \-The thread consists of a map that maps \-Integers to \-Strings. \-The first two entries are the post title (mapped to 1), and the post body (mapped to 2). \-Comments are mapped starting from 3 on.
